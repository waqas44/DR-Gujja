Core Services

  • Cardiac Prevention

    Learning to manage your risk factors for heart disease is important for keeping your heart healthy. Our experts of Cardiologists at NYH& VS help you sustain your cardiac health.

  • Percutaneous Coronary Intervention

    Cardiac Catheterization is a procedure to study or repair the heart by opening blocked arteries.

  • Peripheral Vascular Intervention

    Peripheral vascular intervention is a minimally invasive outpatient (same day) procedure which treats peripheral artery disease by removing the plaque buildup in the arteries.

  • Endovenous Treatment

    We offer patients minimally invasive treatment options to treat chronic venous insufficiency. These procedures are performed by our vascular specialists at our outpatient facilities.

  • Diabetes Prevention

    An estimated 86 million Americans above the age of 20 have prediabetes and 15-30% will develop Type 2 Diabetes within 5 years. At NYH& VS we have an effective Diabetes prevention program.

  • Cardiac Electrophysiology

    At NYH& VS Cardiac Electrophysiologists diagnose patients with atrial fibrillation and treat various cardiac arrhythmias which affects more than 14 million Americans.
